================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Record of Employment (ROE) is a Canadian government form required when an
employee's earnings are interrupted. Workday tracks ROE history for:
- Terminations
- Layoffs
- Leave of absence
- Reduced hours
- Retirement

The ROE is submitted electronically to Service Canada via ROE Web.

Business Process: Review ROE History / Generate ROE
Functional Area: Payroll for Canada
Initiator: Payroll Partner / HR
Typical Duration: 10-20 minutes

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS
================================================================================

1. ROE DATA
   - Employee information
   - Employer information (Business Number)
   - Last day worked
   - First day worked
   - Total insurable earnings
   - Reason for issuing ROE

2. ROE REASONS
   - A - Shortage of work/End of contract
   - D - Illness/Injury
   - E - Quit
   - K - Other
   - M - Dismissal
   - N - Leave of absence
   - P - Parental leave
   - Z - Compassionate care

3. INSURABLE EARNINGS
   - Final pay period(s) breakdown
   - Vacation pay
   - Stat holiday pay
   - Overtime earnings
