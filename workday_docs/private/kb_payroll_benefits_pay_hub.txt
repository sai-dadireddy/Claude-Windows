================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The Benefits and Pay Hub is an employee self-service dashboard in Workday that
provides centralized access to:
- Benefits enrollment and changes
- Pay statements and history
- Tax document access (W-2, W-4)
- Payment elections
- Time off balances
- Compensation information

Business Process: View Benefits and Pay Hub
Functional Area: Employee Self-Service
Initiator: Employee (Worker)
Typical Duration: 5 minutes (review)

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS
================================================================================

1. PAY SECTION
   - Current Pay Statement
   - Pay History
   - Payment Elections
   - Tax Withholding (W-4)
   - Year-end Tax Documents (W-2)

2. BENEFITS SECTION
   - Current Benefit Elections
   - Benefit Costs
   - Dependents/Beneficiaries
   - Open Enrollment Tasks
   - Life Events

3. TIME OFF SECTION
   - Time Off Balances
   - Request Time Off link
   - Absence History

4. RELATED ACTIONS
   - Change Benefits
   - Update Payment Elections
   - View Pay Statements
   - Update Tax Elections

================================================================================
NAVIGATION & TASKS
================================================================================

Access Benefits and Pay Hub (as Employee):
1. Search: "Benefits and Pay Hub"
2. Hub displays personalized dashboard
3. Click sections to expand details

Administrator Test:
1. Log in as employee (or use View As)
2. Navigate to Benefits and Pay Hub
3. Verify all expected sections visible

================================================================================
VALIDATION CHECKLIST
================================================================================

- Hub is accessible to employees
- Pay section shows current/historical pay statements
- Benefits section shows current elections
- Time off balances are accurate
- All expected tasks/links are available
- W-2/W-4 access is available (if configured)
- Mobile-responsive layout works
