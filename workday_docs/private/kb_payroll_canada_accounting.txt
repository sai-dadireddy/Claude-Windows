================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Payroll Accounting configuration in Workday Canada ensures that payroll costs
are posted to the correct general ledger accounts. This includes:
- Earnings account mapping
- Deduction account mapping
- Employer contribution accounts
- Tax liability accounts
- Suspense account monitoring
- Cost center allocation rules

Business Process: Verify Accounting Configuration and Results
Functional Area: Payroll for Canada / Financial Accounting
Initiator: Payroll Partner / Finance
Typical Duration: 30-60 minutes

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS
================================================================================

1. EARNINGS ACCOUNTS
   - Salary expense accounts
   - Hourly wage accounts
   - Overtime expense accounts
   - Bonus expense accounts
   - Commission expense accounts

2. DEDUCTION ACCOUNTS
   - Employee deduction clearing
   - Voluntary deduction liability
   - RRSP liability account
   - Benefits deduction accounts

3. TAX LIABILITY ACCOUNTS
   - Federal income tax payable
   - Provincial income tax payable
   - CPP payable (employee)
   - CPP payable (employer)
   - EI payable (employee)
   - EI payable (employer)

4. EMPLOYER CONTRIBUTION ACCOUNTS
   - CPP employer expense
   - EI employer expense
   - WCB expense accounts
   - Provincial health tax expense
   - Group benefits expense

5. SUSPENSE ACCOUNTS
   - Unmapped earnings suspense
   - Unmapped deductions suspense
   - Error suspense account
   - Clearing accounts

================================================================================
NAVIGATION & TASKS
================================================================================

Review Accounting Configuration:
1. Search: "Maintain Payroll Accounting"
2. Review account mappings
3. Verify cost center rules

View Payroll Postings:
1. Search: "View Payroll Accounting Results"
2. Select pay period
3. Review journal entries before completion

Fix Operational Journals:
1. Search: "Fix Operational Journals With Errors"
2. Review and resolve accounting errors
3. Repost corrected entries

================================================================================
VALIDATION PROCESS
================================================================================

Step 1: Run Pay Calculation
- Calculate payroll for all pay groups
- Review any calculation errors
- Ensure all employees processed

Step 2: Review Accounting Entries
- Check postings before completing payroll
- Verify no suspense account entries
- Confirm cost center allocations

Step 3: Fix Any Errors
- Run "Fix Operational Journals With Errors"
- Update configuration as needed
- Recalculate affected employees

Step 4: Complete and Post
- Complete payroll run
- Verify GL journal entries created
- Reconcile payroll to GL

================================================================================
COMMON ISSUES
================================================================================

- Suspense account postings: Map missing pay codes
- Unbalanced entries: Check rounding rules
- Missing cost centers: Update worktag rules
- Tax account errors: Verify tax liability setup
- Integration errors: Can be ignored until E2E testing
