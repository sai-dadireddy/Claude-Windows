================================================================================

Source: Workday Financial Management
Category: Finance - Ledger Administration
Last Updated: 2025-12-30
Country: CA

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Open Ledger Periods control when journal entries can be posted to specific
accounting periods. For Canadian Payroll:
- Payroll GL entries post to open periods only
- Period must be open for payroll settlement
- Month-end closing requires period closure

Business Process: Verify Open Ledger Periods
Functional Area: Financial Accounting
Initiator: Finance Administrator / Payroll Administrator
Typical Duration: 5-15 minutes

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS
================================================================================

1. LEDGER PERIODS
   - Fiscal year alignment
   - Monthly periods
   - Adjustment periods (13th period)
   - Closing period status

2. PERIOD STATUS
   - Open: Entries allowed
   - Closed: No entries permitted
   - Future: Not yet opened
   - Adjustment: Year-end only

3. LEDGER TYPES
   - Actuals ledger
   - Budget ledger
   - Commitment ledger

================================================================================
ELECTRON AUTOMATION STEPS
================================================================================

Step 1: Access Ledger Period Status
1. enter search box as "View Ledger Period Status"
2. wait for search results
3. click search result containing "View Ledger Period Status"
4. wait for page to load

Step 2: Review Open Periods
5. select ledger as "Actuals"
6. select fiscal year as "{current_year}"
7. wait for periods to load
8. verify current month status is "Open"
9. verify prior month status (Open or Closed)
10. screenshot as "{test_id}_ledger_periods.png"

Step 3: Verify Payroll Period Alignment
11. note the open ledger periods
12. verify payroll payment dates fall within open periods
13. screenshot as "{test_id}_ledger_period_complete.png"

================================================================================
VALIDATION CHECKLIST
================================================================================

- Current payroll period is open
- Prior period open (if retro adjustments needed)
- Future periods not prematurely opened
- Adjustment period closed (except year-end)
- Actuals ledger period status correct
