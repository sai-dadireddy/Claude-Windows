WORKDAY KB ARTICLE: COMPANY TAX REPORTING CONFIGURATION (US)
================================================================================

Source: Workday Payroll Administration
Category: Payroll - Tax Administration
Last Updated: 2025-12-30

================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Company US Tax Reporting configuration defines how a company handles federal,
state, and local tax withholding and reporting. This includes:
- Federal employer identification number (EIN)
- State unemployment insurance (SUI) rates
- Local tax setup and rates
- Supplemental tax methods
- Exempt statuses

Proper configuration ensures accurate tax withholding, correct tax reporting,
and compliance with federal, state, and local tax requirements.

Business Process: View/Edit Company US Tax Reporting
Functional Area: Payroll Administration
Initiator: Payroll Administrator
Typical Duration: 15-30 minutes

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS
================================================================================

1. FEDERAL TAX SETTINGS
   - Federal Employer ID (EIN)
   - Supplemental Tax Method (Flat vs Aggregate)
   - Federal Exempt Status
   - Employer Type (501(c)(3), Government, etc.)
   - FUTA liability settings

2. STATE TAX SETTINGS
   - State Tax ID / Account Number
   - State Unemployment Insurance (SUI) Rate
   - Supplemental Tax Method
   - State Exempt Status
   - Workers' Compensation setup
   - SDI/TDI settings

3. LOCAL TAX SETTINGS
   - Local Tax Authority setup
   - Local Tax IDs
   - Local withholding rates
   - Residency vs work location rules
   - Reciprocity agreements

4. TAX REPORTING ELECTIONS
   - Combined Federal/State filing (EFPS)
   - Electronic filing enrollment
   - Third-party sick pay reporting
   - Quarterly vs annual filing

================================================================================
NAVIGATION & TASKS
================================================================================

View Company US Tax Reporting:
1. Search: "Company US Tax Reporting"
2. Select company
3. Review federal, state, local settings

Edit Company US Tax Reporting:
1. Search: "Edit Company US Tax Reporting"
2. Select company
3. Update tax configuration
4. Submit for approval

Maintain State Tax Configuration:
1. Search: "Maintain US State Tax Configuration"
2. Select state
3. Update SUI rates and settings

================================================================================
VALIDATION CHECKLIST
================================================================================

Federal:
- Federal EIN is correct (9 digits: XX-XXXXXXX)
- Supplemental tax method is appropriate
- Employer type is accurate
- FUTA settings correct

State:
- All operating states are configured
- State tax IDs are valid
- SUI rates are current (update annually)
- Supplemental methods are correct
- State exemptions are documented

Local:
- All applicable local taxes configured
- Local tax IDs are correct
- Withholding rates are current
- Residency rules are accurate
