================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Company CAN Tax Reporting configuration defines how a Canadian company handles
federal and provincial tax withholding and reporting. This includes:
- Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) Business Number
- Provincial tax accounts
- Employment Insurance (EI) rates
- Canada Pension Plan (CPP/QPP) configuration
- Workers' Compensation Board (WCB) rates
- Provincial health tax setup (where applicable)

Proper configuration ensures accurate tax withholding, correct remittances,
and compliance with CRA requirements.

Business Process: View/Edit Company CAN Tax Reporting
Functional Area: Payroll for Canada
Initiator: Payroll Administrator
Typical Duration: 15-30 minutes

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS
================================================================================

1. FEDERAL TAX SETTINGS
   - CRA Business Number (BN - 15 characters)
   - CPP account number (RP program account)
   - EI account number (RP program account)
   - Income tax program account
   - Remittance frequency (monthly, quarterly)

2. PROVINCIAL TAX SETTINGS
   - Provincial business numbers
   - Provincial income tax (Quebec only - Revenu Quebec)
   - QPP (Quebec Pension Plan) for Quebec
   - QPIP (Quebec Parental Insurance Plan)
   - Provincial health taxes (EHT Ontario, etc.)

3. EMPLOYMENT INSURANCE (EI)
   - EI premium rate (standard or reduced)
   - EI reduced rate approval number
   - EI maximum insurable earnings
   - EI employer multiple

4. CANADA PENSION PLAN (CPP/QPP)
   - CPP contribution rate
   - CPP2 enhanced rate (2024+)
   - Maximum pensionable earnings
   - QPP rates for Quebec employees

5. WORKERS' COMPENSATION (WCB)
   - Provincial WCB account numbers
   - Industry classification codes
   - WCB premium rates by province
   - Annual rate verification

6. PROVINCIAL HEALTH TAX
   - Ontario EHT (Employer Health Tax)
   - BC Employer Health Tax
   - Manitoba HE Levy
   - Other provincial levies

NAVIGATION & TASKS
================================================================================

View Company CAN Tax Reporting:
1. Search: "View Company CAN Tax Reporting"
2. Select company
3. Review federal and provincial settings

Edit Company CAN Tax Reporting:
1. Search: "Edit Company CAN Tax Reporting"
2. Select company
3. Update tax configuration
4. Submit for approval

Maintain CAN EI Reduced Rate:
1. Search: "Maintain EI Reduced Rate"
2. Enter reduced rate and approval number
3. Associate with applicable companies

Maintain WCB Configuration:
1. Search: "Maintain Workers Compensation"
2. Select province
3. Update WCB rates and accounts

================================================================================
VALIDATION CHECKLIST
================================================================================

Federal:
- CRA Business Number valid (format: 123456789 RP0001)
- CPP account configured
- EI account configured
- Remittance frequency appropriate
- Income tax program account set

Provincial (each operating province):
- Provincial business numbers valid
- Provincial income tax configured
- Provincial health tax set (if applicable)
- WCB rates current (update annually)
- Quebec: RQ number and QPP/QPIP configured

EI Configuration:
- Standard or reduced rate selected
- Reduced rate approval documented
- EI maximum earnings current
- Employer premium calculation correct

CPP Configuration:
- CPP/CPP2 rates current
- Maximum pensionable earnings set
- Quebec QPP rates configured separately
