OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Time Tracking in Workday allows organizations to capture employee time worked,
manage time entry templates, calculate overtime and other time-based pay,
and integrate with payroll for accurate compensation. The system supports
hourly employees, project-based time tracking, and various time entry methods.

Functional Area: Time Tracking
Primary Roles: Time Tracking Administrator, Timekeeper, Manager, Employee As Self
Related Modules: Payroll, Absence, Projects, General Ledger

Core Components:
- Time Entry Templates: Define how workers enter time
- Time Entry Codes: Categories for time worked (regular, overtime, etc.)
- Time Calculations: Rules for computing pay-related time
- Time Calculation Groups: Collections of related calculations
- Time Period Schedules: Define pay periods and lock dates
- Work Schedule Calendars: Define expected work hours
- Holiday Calendars: Define company holidays

6. wait for report to load
7. verify report displays with no critical errors
8. screenshot as "{TEST_ID}_complete.png"

--------------------------------------

2. wait for search results

4. wait for page to load
5. verify list displays expected items
6. screenshot as "{TEST_ID}_complete.png"

PATTERN 3: Entering Time
------------------------
1. enter search box as "Enter Time"
2. wait for search results
3. click first search result
4. wait for time calendar to load
5. click on date to enter time
6. select time entry code from dropdown
7. enter hours in hours field
8. click button "Submit"
9. wait for submission confirmation
10. verify time entry displays on calendar
11. screenshot as "{TEST_ID}_complete.png"

-----------------------------
1. enter search box as "{Worklet Name}"

================================================================================
ERROR MESSAGES AND TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Common Errors:
- "Worker not eligible for time tracking"
  Solution: Verify time entry template eligibility rules

- "Multiple time entry templates match"
  Solution: Review eligibility rules for overlap

- "Period is locked"
  Solution: Unlock period or contact administrator

- "Maximum hours exceeded"
  Solution: Review validation rules or adjust time entry

- "Required worktag missing"
  Solution: Enter required worktag values
