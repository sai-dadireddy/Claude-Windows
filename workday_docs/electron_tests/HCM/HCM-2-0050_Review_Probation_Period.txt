- Probation Types: Standard, Extended, Performance-Based
- Probation Durations: 30/60/90 days, 3/6 months
- Probation Reasons: New Hire, Transfer, Promotion
- Probation Outcomes: Complete, Extend, Terminate, Change Job

FIELD REFERENCE:
- Worker: Employee prompt field
- Probation Type: Dropdown of configured types
- Probation Start Date: Date picker (usually hire date)
- Probation End Date: Auto-calculated or manual entry
- Probation Duration: Days/Months based on type
- Probation Reason: Dropdown of reasons
- Outcome: Final disposition dropdown
