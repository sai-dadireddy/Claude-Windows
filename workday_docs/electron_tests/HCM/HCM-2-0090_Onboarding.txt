- Emergency Contacts

FIELD REFERENCE (Emergency Contacts):
- Contact Name: Full name of emergency contact
- Relationship: Dropdown (Spouse, Parent, Sibling, Friend, Other)
- Phone Number: Primary contact number
- Alternate Phone: Secondary contact number
- Address: Contact address (optional)

ONBOARDING DASHBOARD ELEMENTS:
- Progress Bar: Shows completion percentage
- Task List: Ordered list of assigned tasks
- Due Dates: Task deadlines
- Status Indicators: Complete/Incomplete/In Progress
- Welcome Message: Customized welcome content
