As a Calibration owner you can verify that any worker changes can be considered in the Calibration process. 
Verify that you have access to the following changes via the "Manage Calibration" button in your inbox.
The effective date of those changes needs to be on or after the start date of the calibration event.

 - Hire
 - Change Job
 - Termination
 - Move Workers by Supervisor Organization
