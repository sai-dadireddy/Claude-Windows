TASK/STEP: Configure and Verify Benefits Waiting Period for New Hires

DESCRIPTION:
Configure waiting period rules that determine when new hires become eligible
for benefits. Verify that new hires are correctly held in waiting status
until eligibility date, then automatically enrolled or notified.

PREREQUISITES:
- User logged in with Benefits Administrator role
- Waiting period rules configured in system
- Test new hire employee to verify waiting period

ELECTRON STEPS:
1. enter search box as "Edit Benefit Waiting Period"
2. wait for search results
3. click search result containing "Edit Benefit Waiting Period"
4. wait for page to load
5. enter waiting period name as "{waiting_period_name}"
6. wait for waiting period details to load
7. select waiting period type as "Days from Hire"
8. enter number of days as "30"
9. select effective date rule as "First of Month After"
10. click button "Submit"
11. wait for confirmation
12. verify message contains "Waiting period updated"
13. screenshot as "BEN-2-0330_config.png"

TEST NEW HIRE WITH WAITING PERIOD:
14. enter search box as "Hire Employee"
15. wait for search results
16. click search result containing "Hire Employee"
17. wait for page to load
18. enter hire date as "{hire_date}"
19. enter worker details as required
20. complete hire process through submission
21. verify hire completes successfully
22. screenshot as "BEN-2-0330_hired.png"

VERIFY WAITING PERIOD STATUS:
23. enter search box as "View Worker"
24. wait for search results
25. click search result containing "View Worker"
26. enter worker as "{new_hire_employee_id}"
27. click link "Benefits" tab
28. verify benefits status shows "In Waiting Period"
29. verify eligibility date displays correctly
30. verify calculated eligibility date = first of month after 30 days
31. screenshot as "BEN-2-0330_waiting.png"

VERIFY ELIGIBILITY AFTER WAITING PERIOD:
32. enter search box as "Preview Benefit Eligibility"
33. wait for search results
34. click search result containing "Preview Benefit Eligibility"
35. enter worker as "{new_hire_employee_id}"
36. enter effective date as "{eligibility_date}"
37. click button "OK"
38. verify employee shows as eligible for benefits
39. verify enrollment event created for new hire
40. screenshot as "BEN-2-0330_eligible.png"

VERIFICATION:
- [ ] Waiting period configuration saved
- [ ] New hire completed successfully
- [ ] Employee shows "In Waiting Period" status
- [ ] Calculated eligibility date is correct
- [ ] Employee becomes eligible after waiting period
- [ ] New hire enrollment event created
- [ ] No error messages displayed

EXPECTED RESULT:
New hire waiting period correctly enforced, employee held from benefits
until eligibility date, then enrollment event triggered automatically.

COMMON WAITING PERIOD CONFIGURATIONS:
- First of Month after 30 days
- First of Month after 60 days
- First of Month after 90 days
- Date of Hire (no waiting period)
- First of Next Month
- Specific calendar date

NOTES:
- ACA requires waiting period not exceed 90 days for health plans
- Different waiting periods may apply to different plan types
- Rehires may have adjusted or waived waiting periods
- COBRA coverage not subject to waiting periods
- Source: [KB Benefits Enrollment, WSDL Analysis]

API ALTERNATIVE:
- SOAP: Put_Benefit_Waiting_Period, Hire_Employee
- REST: /benefitsAdmin/v1/waitingPeriods
