TASK/STEP: Configure Benefit Group Eligibility Rules

DESCRIPTION:
Configure eligibility rules that determine which employees are assigned to
specific benefit groups. Benefit groups control which plans are available
to employees based on job classification, location, and other criteria.

PREREQUISITES:
- User logged in with Benefits Administrator role
- Benefit groups already created
- Understanding of eligibility criteria requirements

7. scroll to eligibility rules section
8. click button "Edit Eligibility Rules"
9. wait for eligibility rule editor to load
10. click button "Add Condition"
11. select condition type as "Employee Type"
12. select operator as "In"
13. select values as "Regular", "Full-Time"
14. click button "OK"
