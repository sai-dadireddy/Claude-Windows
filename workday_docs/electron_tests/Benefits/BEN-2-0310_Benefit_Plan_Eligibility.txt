TASK/STEP: Configure Benefit Plan Eligibility Rules

DESCRIPTION:
Configure eligibility rules for specific benefit plans within a benefit group.
Plan-level eligibility provides finer control over who can enroll in specific
plans based on additional criteria like age, tobacco use, or tenure.

PREREQUISITES:
- User logged in with Benefits Administrator role
- Benefit plans already created
- Benefit groups configured
- Understanding of plan eligibility requirements

COMMON PLAN ELIGIBILITY RULES:
- Minimum/Maximum Age
- Minimum Service Period (months/years)
- Minimum Hours per Week
- Full-Time/Part-Time Status
- Tobacco User Status (for premium tier)
- Wellness Program Participation
- Geographic Location
- Evidence of Insurability Requirement

NOTES:
- Plan eligibility is evaluated after group eligibility
- Age calculated as of effective date
- Service calculated from hire date
- Consider Evidence of Insurability (EOI) requirements
