DESCRIPTION:
Create a statistic entry to record non-monetary data for allocation bases,
KPI tracking, or operational metrics. Statistics are used to allocate costs
based on headcount, square footage, units produced, or other operational data.

PREREQUISITES:
- User logged in with Accountant or Finance Manager role
- Statistic worktag types configured
- Target company has open accounting period
- Allocation rules referencing statistics (if applicable)

ELECTRON STEPS:
1. enter search box as "Create Statistic"
2. wait for search results
3. click search result containing "Create Statistic"
4. wait for page to load
5. select Company as "{test_company}"
6. enter Accounting Date as "{current_period_date}"
7. select Statistic Type as "{statistic_type}"
8. enter Memo as "Statistic entry - FACC-4-0170"
9. click button "Add"
10. wait for statistic line section
11. click link "Add Statistic Line"
12. select Cost Center as "{cost_center}"
13. enter Statistic Amount as "{statistic_value}"
14. select Unit of Measure as "{uom}"
15. enter Line Memo as "Monthly headcount"
16. click button "OK"
17. click link "Add Statistic Line"
18. select Cost Center as "{cost_center_2}"
19. enter Statistic Amount as "{statistic_value_2}"
20. select Unit of Measure as "{uom}"
21. enter Line Memo as "Monthly headcount"
22. click button "OK"
23. click button "Submit"
24. wait for confirmation
25. verify message contains "submitted"
26. screenshot as "FACC-4-0170_complete.png"
