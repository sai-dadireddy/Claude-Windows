TEST NAME: Review Period Schedule Configuration
CONFIDENCE: [HIGH] Score: 8.5/10

================================================================================

DESCRIPTION:
Verify that all expected period schedules are present in the tenant, dates are correct (period start, period end and payment date) and that forward accrual settings are as expected. Verify that period schedules are linked to Absence / Time Tracking as applicable. Ensure that that the correct number of periods are in place for each year.

WORKDAY ROLE: Payroll Administrator
EST. DURATION: 5 minutes

PREREQUISITES:
- User logged in with Payroll Administrator permissions
- Access to View Period Schedule task
- Knowledge of expected period schedule names and configurations

ELECTRON STEPS:
1. enter search box as "View Period Schedule"
2. wait for search results
3. click search result containing "View Period Schedule"
4. wait for page to load
5. select period schedule dropdown as "{schedule_name}"
6. wait for periods to load
7. verify period table displays periods
8. scroll to view all periods
9. verify period dates are consecutive with no gaps or overlaps
10. verify payment dates fall on valid business days
11. click related actions menu
12. click link "View Details"
13. verify forward accrual settings are displayed
14. verify Absence/Time Tracking links are configured
15. verify correct number of periods per year (Weekly=52, Bi-weekly=26, Semi-monthly=24, Monthly=12)
16. screenshot as "USP-5-0010_period_schedule.png"

VERIFICATION:
- [ ] All expected period schedules are present in the tenant
- [ ] Period dates are correct (period start, period end, payment date)
- [ ] Forward accrual settings match expected configuration
- [ ] Period schedules linked to Absence/Time Tracking as applicable
- [ ] Correct number of periods exist for each year
- [ ] No gaps or overlaps in period dates
- [ ] Payment dates fall on valid business days

PERIOD SCHEDULE COMPONENTS TO VERIFY:
- Period ID (unique identifier)
- Period Start Date
- Period End Date
- Payment Date (check date)
- Accounting Date Override (for GL posting)
- Forward Accrual Indicator
- Continuous Payroll Calculation settings

RELATED REPORTS:
- All Period Schedules
- Period Schedule Details
- Pay Group by Period Schedule
- Payroll Calendar Report

API ALTERNATIVE:
SOAP:
  Service: Payroll
  Operations: Get_Period_Schedules

REST:
  Endpoint: /payroll/v2/periodSchedules
  Method: GET
