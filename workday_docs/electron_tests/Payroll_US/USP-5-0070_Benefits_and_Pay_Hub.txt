DESCRIPTION:
As an Employee as Self worker. Ensure Benefits and Pay Hub is available to employee and contains the appropriate tasks/reports

WORKDAY ROLE: Employee (Self-Service) / Payroll Administrator for validation
EST. DURATION: 5 minutes

PREREQUISITES:
- User logged in as Employee (or using View As Employee)
- Employee has active payroll and benefits
- Benefits and Pay Hub configured in tenant

ELECTRON STEPS:
1. enter search box as "Benefits and Pay Hub"
2. wait for search results
3. click search result containing "Benefits and Pay Hub"
4. wait for page to load
5. verify section "Pay" is visible
6. verify link "View Pay Statements" is available
7. verify link "Payment Elections" is available
8. screenshot as "USP-5-0070_pay_section.png"
9. verify section "Benefits" is visible
10. verify current benefit elections are displayed
11. verify link "Change Benefits" is available
12. screenshot as "USP-5-0070_benefits_section.png"
13. verify section "Time Off" is visible
14. verify time off balances are displayed
15. screenshot as "USP-5-0070_time_off_section.png"
16. click link "View Pay Statements"
17. wait for pay statements page to load
18. verify pay statement list appears
19. navigate back to Benefits and Pay Hub
20. screenshot as "USP-5-0070_complete.png"

VERIFICATION:
- [ ] Benefits and Pay Hub is accessible to employee
- [ ] Pay section shows current/historical pay statements access
- [ ] Benefits section shows current benefit elections
- [ ] Time off balances are displayed accurately
- [ ] All expected tasks/links are available
- [ ] View Pay Statements link works correctly
- [ ] Payment Elections link is accessible
- [ ] Change Benefits link is accessible

HUB COMPONENTS TO VERIFY:
- Pay Section: Pay Statements, Payment Elections, Tax Documents
- Benefits Section: Current Elections, Benefit Costs, Dependents
- Time Off Section: Balances, Request Time Off
- Related Actions: Change Benefits, Update Payments, View Pay

RELATED REPORTS:
- Employee Self-Service Usage Report
- Benefits Enrollment Summary
- Pay Statement Distribution Report
- Time Off Balance Report

API ALTERNATIVE:
SOAP:
  Service: Human_Resources
  Operations: Get_Workers (with benefits response group)

REST:
  Endpoint: /payroll/v2/workers/{workerId}/payStatements
  Method: GET
