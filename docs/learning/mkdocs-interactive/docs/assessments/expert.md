## Part 4: Systems Thinking (20 points)

### Real-World System Design (20 points)

**Scenario**: You're the lead AI engineer at a healthcare startup. Design an AI-powered diagnostic assistant:

**Requirements**:
- Multimodal (medical images + clinical notes + patient history)
- 1M+ patients, 10M+ medical records
- <3s diagnosis suggestion
- 99.5% accuracy requirement
- HIPAA compliant
- $10K/month budget
- Works offline in rural clinics (edge deployment)

**Deliverable**: Complete design document (5-8 pages):

1. **System Architecture** (5 points)
   - High-level architecture diagram
   - Component breakdown
   - Data flow diagrams

2. **Technology Stack** (5 points)
   - Model selection with justification
   - Infrastructure choices (cloud, edge, hybrid)
   - Database and vector store selection
   - Monitoring and observability stack

3. **Implementation Strategy** (5 points)
   - Fine-tuning approach (datasets, methods)
   - Retrieval strategy (hybrid search, reranking)
   - Caching and optimization
   - Security and compliance measures

4. **Operations & Maintenance** (5 points)
   - Deployment strategy (blue-green, canary)
   - Monitoring and alerting
   - Cost breakdown and optimization
   - Scalability plan
   - Disaster recovery

**Grading**:
- (18-20) Production-ready design, exceptional
- (15-17) Strong design, minor improvements needed
- (10-14) Good design, needs refinement
- (0-9) Incomplete or incorrect
